When Fate Fits

When an act of misfortune happens to a 14 year old guy in which he gets separated from his parents in the middle of ocean all then he holds upon is hope and belief in fate. In the way of finding them he comes across a lot of difficulties. But rather than loosing hope he withstands everything fate brings to him . He earns some money while working at an old woman’s house. He travels from place to place in search of his parents .He on a task given by an old man intentionally fails to find his parents but unintentionally he gets know from a woman about his parents. His normal part of life starts .

Who is Hashmat?

Hashmat javaid a 18 year old literature student who found the true peace in the relationship with paper and pen. He took literature as a stream so that he can learn to appreciate words and their power. To inspire people to do better things has always been his passion But because of not being a very good speaker , Becoming a writer was the only option . From writing quotes ,doing poetry on Youtube and writing his debut novel ‘WHEN FATE FITS’ , he has came a long way . Hashmat is a firm believer in the power of paper and the words written on it to change the mindset of people . On investing his conscience to the writing , He is a writer by profession and a student by passion.
